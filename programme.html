Lightning Talks

Grab your seats and hold on tight for Lightning Talks will bring you on an adventure like never before. Just within a span of 5 minutes or so, our speakers will take your hand and guide you through different topics such as concepts, events, projects and more! Each talk is equipped with tons of information and packs a punch. Focus and listen, you just might be able to catch that lightning when it flashes!

Introduction To A Level Computing

I can show you the worl-...ahem*. I can show you A-level Computing in JC. Grab your seats for our speaker will bring you on a journey to show you what computing in JC is about.

By: Miss Koh Yi Ting

Detecting content modifications in photographs

Hmm..so similar yet its clearly different. Real or Fake? Join us to find out how to differentiate images using various algorithms.

By: Mr Peirce Chow Zheng Jie

Project Showcase

"Alas! It is finished! My masterpiece is finally done!" The Project Showcase features a gallery of python-related projects skillfully devised and coded by our students. These projects are made to solve real-world problems and to achieve one of many goals, namely, to save the world through technology. Enjoy our students masterpieces and feel free to consult them on their projects.

DHS: SocialShield

Profile page rampant with vulgarities? Social media page screaming obscenities that makes your skin crawl and makes you want to puke? Have no fear! Introducing the new and unique online protection, SocialShield! Its not an antivirus but it will detect and notify you if there are harmful content on the social site you're on. Once implemented, this intelligent programme automatically sieves through the content on the page and warns you about the danger on that page. Ain't that neat? *clunk* Behold, the SocialShield, the mighty blockade against harmful content.

By:
Mr Chan Ger Hean
Mr Lee Wei Jie
Mr Yu Shi Jie

DHS: RPiCam

I spy with my little eye....there you are! Gotcha! Ever wanted your own CCTV camera to survey or watch over your belongings? Well fret no longer for we will show you how to create one by yourself, simply by just using Raspberry Pi and the Telegram App! You can control it remotely through the app allowing you to adjust the angle and view from where you like the most!
~I have Raspberry Pi, I have Telegram. Ungh! RPiCam!~

By:
Mr Peirce Chow Zheng Jie
